Overview

As a world-class materials and energy solution provider, Samsung SDI continues its focus on R&D investment to secure new technologies and boosts technological competitiveness. In addition, by expanding our business to include cutting-edge materials and parts manufacturing, Samsung SDI is now making a leap forward to becoming the solution provider for batteries and materials for IT and vehicles, as well as energy sector. In particular, the year 2014 was a cornerstone year for Samsung SDI since its R&D Center and Electronics Materials Business joined Samsung Future Technology Campus to create synergy through joint research and development activities with Samsung affiliates. We promise to continue our best efforts to maintain our global leadership in technology, capitalizing upon our technical edge and capability in materials and pursuing the future energy solution business.

How do we create value?

  • R&D Investments Samsung SDI’s continued and solid investments in R&D serve to enhance its competitive edge. As of 2021, the annual R&D spending was KRW 877.648 billion, which accounted for 7.39% of revenue.
  • R&D Hub Samsung SDI’s R&D Hub consists of Samsung SDI R&D Center, Small-Sized Li-ion Battery Business, Automotive & ESS Battery Business, and Electronic Materials Business. These networks of R&D teams work together to boost Samsung SDI’s global leadership in technology. The efforts continue in researching and developing battery materials and securing a stable supply of materials (e.g. import from Chile).
  • Researchers and Developers As of late 2021, Samsung SDI retains approximately 2,300 R&D experts and 53% of them are either Masters or PhD graduates. Samsung SDI operates a learning system where employees share their experience and knowledge. By the same token, in order to further nurture its global talent, the company has increased its support through various systems and contents including academic training programs, in-house technology college system, global talent enabling centers, global educational portals, and outstanding educational contents.

R&D Areas

Battery
Establish Materials R&D Center’s Mid/Long-Term Strategies
Samsung SDI has established and has been operating mid/long-term strategies in order to secure product leadership. Building upon such strategies, we plan to implement a big-data-based system that integrates scientific technology and HR management, to create a ‘Strong and Lively R&D Center.’
Strengthen R&D Partnerships
In order to take the lead in the ever-changing technology and market environment, Samsung SDI has been amping up its R&D partnerships. Samsung SDI conducts joint R&D projects with universities and research institutes home and abroad, to develop innovative elementary technologies and capitalize on them in the market. In addition, the company continues to expand its overseas R&D networks to build a system where all pro­cesses from development to completion can be locally managed. At the same time, Samsung SDI concentrates on technologies for cutting-edge battery materials at the ‘Samsung Future Technology Campus’ where collaboration with affiliated companies generates tremendous synergy.
Bolster R&D in Li-ion Battery Materials
Recently, the focus of Li-ion battery market has moved from small-sized lithium-ion bat­teries onto medium-to-large lithium-ion batteries for automotive and energy storage system applications. With the increasing market demand for electric vehicles featuring longer range and better battery systems, Samsung SDI is committed to align itself with such need by working on technologies that can overcome limitations of the existing materials. As more and more devices are built with Li-ion batteries and the competition intensifies, we are raising our efforts in R&D to upgrade the existing materials and develop new materials with high-capacity thereby coming up with longer battery life and shorter charging time.
Materials
Electronics Materials
Samsung SDI strives to develop core materials for high-tech IT products such as semiconductors, displays, new energy solutions in line with market need. We spare no efforts in developing competitive products built with innovative technology and top quality. The achievements made by Samsung SDI include development of polarizing PET films, high-efficiency electrode pastes, OLED evaporation materials with higher efficiency and longer life, and the next-generation CR materials for high-resolution displays. We will keep on coming up with innovations in OLED materials, polarizing films, high-brightness CRs, and even more with the aim of expanding our market leadership in both existing and new markets.

Patents

Creating synergy for intellectual property

Samsung SDI has built a foundation to demonstrate synergy not only in battery materials but also in future energy materials. In particular, we are pioneering potential business fields with competitive patents, in the backdrop of intensive support for major projects aimed at the next-generation battery and materials. As of late 2021, Samsung SDI maintains 17,735 patents registered home and abroad. Especially, we have secured 10,428 registered patents in major markets such as the US, Europe, and China, and are
strengthening our responsiveness therein.
