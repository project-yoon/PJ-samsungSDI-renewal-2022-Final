Materials & Battery
Cell Development

We develop next-generation materials that will lead innovation as well as materials that play a crucial role in batteries and electronics materials.

Materials & battery cell

We develop next-generation materials that will lead innovation as well as materials that play a crucial role in batteries and electronics materials.

Roles
Developing battery materials
Develop four major components of a lithium-ion battery - cathode, anode, electrolyte, and separator that makes possible battery features of high capacity, high efficiency, and quick charge.
Develop new materials and elementary technologies for the next-generation batteries.
Developing battery models
In charge of creating composition and structure design of electrodes that enable battery features of high capacity and high efficiency.
Conduct advanced development and verification&validation of electrodes for the next-generation batteries.
Generate, select, and implement product-specific requirements.
Developing battery structures&components
Design and develop elementary technologies for new battery structures.
In charge of designing, conducting simulations, and developing battery structures and parts
Developing electronics materials
Develop display materials such as polarizing films for TVs and mobile devices, adhesives for foldable displays, evaporation materials for OLED, process materials for QD, etc., as well as next-generation materials.
Develop semiconductor materials for processes such as lithography, CMP, wet etch, cleaning, packaging, image sensor, etc.
Develop optimal materials via design, synthesis, polymerization, reforming, and mix of organic&inorganic materials.
Assessing and analyzing electronics materials
Conduct performance assessment on materials for displays and semiconductors.
Develop replication analysis and quality evaluation methods mirroring customers’ processes, as well as defect root cause analysis and identification of corrective measures.
In charge of structure analysis, property analysis, and development of advanced analysis methods
Requirements
  • Degree in or relating to chemistry, chemical engineering, materials engineering, metallurgical engineering, fiber and polymer engineering, mechanical engineering, mathematics, and/or physics
  • Knowledge in basic principles of batteries and electronics materials as well as materials and cell development
  • Certifications or experience in the related fields(projects, dissertations, patents, contests, etc.)

  • Knowledge in mechanical engineering, modeling, and simulation of battery modules and packs and battery circuit architecture
