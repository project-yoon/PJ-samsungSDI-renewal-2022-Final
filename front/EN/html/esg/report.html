Sustainability Reports 2021

Since 2003, Samsung SDI has been released Sustainability Reports to disclose how we have made our business more sustainable as well as our endeavor to grow together with all the stakeholders. Samsung SDI published 19th Sustainability Report in 2021. We have analyzed issues, risks, and opportunities with regards to sustainability management and come up with optimal strategies for sustainability management.
