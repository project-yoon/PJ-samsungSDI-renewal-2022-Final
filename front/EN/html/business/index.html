IT Device

We build laptop batteries with high capacity cell technology and optimum pack development & production technology,
thereby enhancing the performance and user mobility and allowing for even slimmer and lighter laptops.

Samsung SDI’s cutting-edge cell technology and outstanding battery pack design build ultra-thin and ultra-light lithium-ion batteries that make possible even thinner and lighter tablets.

Samsung SDI’s cell technology to build batteries with high energy density and outstanding pack technology bring longer battery life and thinner and lighter design to mobile phones, meeting ever-rising customer expectations.

Samsung SDI will unlock the limitless potential of the wearable device market with its lithium-ion battery technology that prides in having the highest density and its capability of making flat batteries and curved batteries which are the optimal choice for wearable devices.

Samsung SDI will make it possible to go mobile anytime and anywhere by providing lithium-ion batteries with the highest capacity, quality and safety. Our battery cells and packs with multi-specification adaptability will give maximum boost to your power bank products.

Power Device

Samsung SDI’s battery cells offers the highest capacity and output. We also pride in having a complete and thorough process of verifying the safety of high-performance battery packs, which will ensure lighter and stronger power tools.

Samsung SDI’s Li-ion battery will help improve the convenience and performance of garden tools with its high energy efficiency, density, and longer battery life. Since electric-powered garden tools run quiet, have low environmental impact and cause no harm to human health, our Li-ion battery solution guarantees all the more convenient and pleasant garden tool experience.

Samsung SDI’s technological edge in Li-ion batteries ensures stable and high suction power that lasts. With the energy density five times higher and double the battery life compared to those of other players in the field, our solutions enable compact yet powerful vacuum cleaners.

Tran Device

Samsung SDI provides high capacity 3.5Ah cells for E-bikes which make the battery-powered mobility go longer and improve design flexibility. With our Li-ion batteries, your E-bike experience will leap to the next level.

Samsung SDI’s Li-ion battery solution offers much faster charging and even more battery life compared to conventional lead-acid batteries. This feature ensures more convenient maintenance and lower costs. An eco-friendly and economical solution for E-scooters is what our Li-ion battery packs are here to offer.

Samsung SDI ‘s high efficiency Li-ion batteries ensure more compact and longer-lasting ignition. Our batteries have high peak discharge and are built without hazardous and dangerous materials such as cadmium, lead, mercury so that your mobility can start quickly and superbly while reducing environmental impact.
