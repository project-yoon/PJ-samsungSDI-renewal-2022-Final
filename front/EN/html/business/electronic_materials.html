Electronic Materials

Beyond the naked eye
underlies the power
of digital transformation

  • Small-Sized
    Lithium-ion Battery
  • Mid to Large
    sized Battery
  • Electronic
    Materials
  • Important
    Safety Information
  • In 1994, Samsung SDI took the first step into electronic
    material business with development of EMC, an encapsulant for the
    semiconductor. We have been building top-class competence
    and technology in semiconductor, display and battery businesses and,
    as a result, Samsung SDI has been maintaining its competitive advantage
    in the global market. Our consistent drive in investing
    in research and development of OLED materials, in particular, has resulted in
    successful production of electron transport layer (ETL) and development
    of phosphorescence green host (PGH).
  • Samsung SDI also affords the best effort to grow its competence
    in separators, a key material for batteries. We are very much poised to
    maintain the position as a market leader, by bringing next-generation
    materials to the market.

Application

  • Semiconductor
  • Display
Display
1. Micro-pattern formation

Samsung SDI leverages its expertise in organic and inorganic materials design so as to provide materials solutions for semiconductor business that requires high-performing and highly integrated memory and process chip products for cutting-edge devices such as mobile phones and laptops.

2. Defect reduction

A race to reduce nanometers in semiconductor chips is revealing defects that have been avoiding detection before. Samsung SDI undertakes to eliminate such defects with its superb technology in refining and defect control.

3. Excellent stability

Stability comes into play as semiconductor device functions keep stacking. Samsung SDI’s exceptional competence in composition design and reliability assurance technology makes it possible to provide differentiated solutions for protecting semiconductor device from external impact, moisture, or damage, ensuring that the semiconductor device runs stably.

4. Portfolio

SOH, SOD, EMC, CMP Slurry, CIS PR, VS, EUV PR, EUV UL, EUV Thinner/developer

Semiconductor
1. Low power consumption

A booming trend of people enjoying big size contents such as live streaming and gaming means that displays are consuming more electricity. Samsung SDI’s technology and design implement high efficiency and transmittance in the materials, reducing power consumption of display products.

2. Excellent display resolution

With superior materials and physical property designs, Samsung SDI takes pride in supplying only the most superb materials featuring excellent viewing angle, contrast ratio, and purity.

3. Diverse form-factors

Rich data base and technology expertise of Samsung SDI serve to broaden the spectrum of display form-factors from market-proven flexible displays and foldable displays to upcoming slidable and rollable displays.

4. Portfolio

Polarizing film (LCD, OLED), FOCA, OLED evaporation material (G-Host, ETL, a-ETL, p-dopant), TFE, QD-Ink, etc
