Automotive Battery

Battery is the key to sustainable innovation in automobiles

    Lithium-ion Battery

  • The world is embracing a paradigm shift of mobility,
    from internal combustion engines to electric drive. In such ever-changing
    environment, Samsung SDI leverages its abundance of experience
    and expertise accumulated in mobile device battery busines
    in order to develop highly efficient and most energy-dense batteries
    catered to electric vehicles.
  • With such relentless investment in R&D, Samsung SDI keeps
    bringing originality and innovation to the automotive business scene
    as a market leader.

Application

  • Pure Electric Vehicle
  • PHEV
  • Mid-HEV
Pure Electric Vehicle

Samsung SDI has been supplying battery cells with the highest volumetric energy density bar-none for global car makers in Europe and the US. An endless streak of Samsung SDI’s innovation in automotive batteries for all-electric vehicles enables battery products to have longer durability and higher capacity.

PHEV

Just like EV applications, our standardized PHEV battery cell format produces better driving range under the same exterior dimensions of a vehicle which manages more-than 50km electric-only driving without changing the packaging dimensions for the next generation application.

Mid-HEV

As the next generation mobility from internal combustion engine vehicles, micro/mild hybrid electric vehicles feature engine stop/start functionality. Vehicles equipped with a micro/mild hybrid system maintains the power supply voltage below 60V thereby incorporating advantages of engine stop/start system and hybrid electric vehicles. With superb output and improved durability, Samsung SDI’s “HI Cap” battery cells help maximize the fuel saving potential.
